WHAT MAKES OUR BREADS SPECIAL?

We reject commercial yeast. Our bread is hand-kneaded using the best organic ingredients and ferments slowly for 14 to 17 hours. This commitment breaks down the gluten, giving you a flavorful, solar-baked loaf that is naturally much easier to digest than rushed store-bought bread.
